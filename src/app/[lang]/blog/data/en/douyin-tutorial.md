# Tutorial on downloading videos from douyin

> Web-based operation process:
>
> 1. Get the URL link from social media
> 2. Paste the URL link of social media into the website input box
> 3. Download pictures or videos to local

## 1. How to get the media URL link from douyin?

- Find the share button on the right side of the video you want to download and click on the copy link

## 2. Paste the link into [SnapVee](https://snapvee.com/)[ ](http://snapvee.com/blog/tutorial) website input box

## 3. Download pictures or videos to local

- Click Download, wait for the video to be parsed successfully, click Download Video in the pop-up window, and save the video locally.
